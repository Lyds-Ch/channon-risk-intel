Our Services

Ethical, intelligent, and practical online safety solutions.

1. Online Safety Education & Workshops

We deliver tailored workshops and public talks to help individuals, families, and organisations better understand how to stay safe online.

  • Understanding the risks of online content (hate speech, misinformation, grooming, etc.)
  • Helping parents understand what their children may be exposed to
  • Building digital literacy and critical thinking skills
  • Recognising algorithmic influence on what we see online

Delivered in schools, community settings, and corporate training environments.

2. AI-Powered Moderation Tools & Classifier Development

We develop custom content classifiers using NLP and machine learning to detect:

  • Hate speech and coded language
  • Extremist narratives and policy-evasive rhetoric
  • Misinformation and online manipulation

Our tools are designed with ethical safeguards and can be tailored for researchers, NGOs, and moderation teams.

3. Custom Monitoring Dashboards & Data Tools

We build backend solutions for:

  • Scraping and analysing public social media content (e.g., YouTube, X/Twitter)
  • Extracting and visualising comment sections and user behaviour
  • Cross-platform risk tracking and engagement mapping
  • Supporting responsible data collection and insights

4. Risk Intelligence & Strategic Advisory

We work with clients to understand emerging online threats and platform risks by offering:

  • Research-backed risk assessments and strategic reports
  • Guidance on algorithmic harms and governance
  • Policy recommendations for online safety and ethical AI
  • Support for CVE (Countering Violent Extremism) and online harms teams
